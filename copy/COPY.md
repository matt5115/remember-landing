### Section headline (H2)

### Step 1
**📝 Create your Remember list**  
You'll build one specific list in your Reminders app — named exactly "Remember." That's the system. Siri uses it as a memory bank, not a to-do list.

### Step 2
**🎙️ Tell Siri what to remember**  
Any time something matters — a name, a code, something someone tells you — you say it out loud. "Hey Siri, add to the Remember list — the neighbor's dog is named Biscuit." That's it. No typing.

### Step 3
**💬 Ask Siri anytime**
