## Headline Chosen: Option 2

> **Outsource your mom-brain. Finally.**
> *You're already holding everything together — now let your iPhone hold the details for you.*

**Why:** "Finally" signals relief, not tech. "Outsource" is modern and slightly cheeky — it's a word moms use for getting a cleaner, getting groceries delivered — it lands immediately. Zero creep factor. Warm, direct, not scary. The rejected line leaned mysterious ("your iPhone knows something it shouldn't") — this one leans empowering.

## Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Hero background | Deep dark raspberry-to-black gradient | #2E0D1E → #1A0A06 |
| Primary CTA | Hot pink | #FF3881 |
| Accent / highlight | Electric yellow | #FFD23F |
| Accent warm | Coral | #FF5C3A |
| Text light | Warm cream | #FFF8F3 |
| Secondary backgrounds | Deep dark plum | #1C0A14 |

**Why:** Research said "electric cobalt + cream OR rich raspberry + bone" — we went raspberry + cream with electric yellow punches. High contrast. TikTok-native energy. The hot pink CTA is impossible to miss on mobile. Electric yellow pulls headlines and price points. The deep dark backgrounds make sections feel like intentional blocks, not generic grey alternating rows. No sage green anywhere near this page.

---

## Typography

- **Font:** Plus Jakarta Sans (Google Fonts CDN)
- **Headlines:** 900 weight, tight kerning, em-size leading
- **Body:** 400–600 weight, clean, readable
- **Rationale:** Research specifically called out Plus Jakarta Sans. Bold display energy, feels modern and warm, not corporate.
